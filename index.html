EmaChat is known for its private communication that has no security.

That is how your online privacy is safe and secure. But, a question arises: can anyone hack into your EmaChat? If they want to, they also need a unique identifier to track you at first. In EmaChat, you have no unique identity. So it is not possible to hack your EmaChat.

EmaChat strongly believes that - 'We need a secure place for our special one. Because there should be no compromise when it comes to the safety and security of your loved one.'
